Version 0.5 - Created on:

Summary of Changes

  • Play Sound on Click: Sounds start playing when the corresponding icon is clicked.
  • Pulsating Icon: The icon pulsates at 117 BPM while the sound is playing.
  • Waiting Animation: If a sound is clicked while another is playing, the new sound waits until the next loop to start, indicated by a smooth up-and-down animation.
  • Syncing Sounds: All sounds start together and remain synchronized.
  • 8-Dot Meter Animation: An animation of 8 dots indicates the measure of the file.
